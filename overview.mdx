[Hindsight](https://www.usehindsight.com) is an AI platform for win-loss analysis and competitive intelligence. 
Use AI to understand exactly why your team wins or loses deals - then turn those insights into action.

Hindsight helps teams track better win-loss reasons, conduct win-loss interviews, and understand the competitive landscape.
Use this data to make informed decisions about your competitive positioning, pricing, sales process, and product.
