### Why Hindsight?
<Steps>
  <Step title="Never Miss an Important Update">
    Ensure your team is well-informed of competitor activities and product launches.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Become Market Experts">
    Hindsight helps your team deeply understand your competitors and their products, allowing you to be a better partner for prospects.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Data-Driven Positioning">
    Hindsight analyzes your deals to understand why you win and lose against your competitors. Use data to drive your positioning, roadmap, and messaging.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save Time, Win More">
    Hindsight automates the entire Competitive Intelligence workflow, ensuring your team is in the best position to win competitive deals.
  </Step>
</Steps>
