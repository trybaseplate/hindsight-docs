<Steps>
  <Step icon="plug" title="Connect Integrations">
    Connect your sales tools to automatically import deals, calls, and customer
    data. Hindsight integrates with all major CRM and sales platforms. **Popular
    Integrations:** 
    - **Salesforce or HubSpot**: Automatic deal import and
    analysis. See [Salesforce Integration](/integrations/salesforce-integration) [HubSpot Integration](/integrations/hubspot-integration) 
    - **Gong, Clari, Outreach, Fathom**: Sales call transcription and insights. See [Gong Integration](/integrations/gong) 
    {/* <img alt="Integrations" classname="block" src="/images/integrations.png" /> */}
  </Step>

  <Step icon="sliders" title="Customize Analysis">
    Tailor Hindsight's analysis to match your business needs.
    
    **Add Custom Answers & Metrics from Library:**
    - Browse our pre-built library of answers and metrics
    - Select the insights most relevant to your sales process and win-loss objectives
    - Customize how data is extracted and displayed
    
    **Optional: Add Your Own Tags (We'll Auto-Detect):**
    - **[Features](https://app.usehindsight.com/settings/features)**: Define product capabilities to track across deals
    - **[Decision Drivers](https://app.usehindsight.com/settings/reasons)**: Set up factors that influence buying decisions
    - **[Competitors](https://app.usehindsight.com/settings/competitors)**: Add competitors for automatic mention detection
    
    Hindsight will automatically detect and tag these elements in your deal conversations.
    {/* <img alt="Customize Analysis" classname="block" src="/images/customize-analysis.png" /> */}
  </Step>

  <Step icon="wand-magic-sparkles" title="Run Recipes & Create Workflows">
    Put your insights into action with recipes and automated workflows.
    
    **Recipes:**
    - Run pre-built analysis recipes on your deals
    - Generate battlecards, reports, and competitive insights
    - Extract specific data points across multiple deals
    
    **Workflows:**
    - Automate recurring analysis tasks
    - Set up triggers based on deal events
    - Schedule regular reports and updates
    {/* <img alt="Recipes and Workflows" classname="block" src="/images/recipes-workflows.png" /> */}
  </Step>
</Steps>
