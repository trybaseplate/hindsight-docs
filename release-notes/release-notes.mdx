## July 2026

The first stable release of the Hindsight MCP server, a guided setup flow for connecting your CRM and call intelligence tools, smarter "no evidence" handling in Custom Metrics, and a fix for deal exports.

  **`upload_to_knowledge_base`** — add your own docs, playbooks, case studies, or approved messaging.

### Metrics Enhancements
We've made several enhancements to our metrics capabilities to provide you with deeper insights and more flexibility in how you evaluate deals.

####  Metrics Scoring Guidelines
You can now choose documents to use as guidelines for the model to reference when scoring a deal. For example, when scoring sales execution,
you might want to provide your sales playbook or coaching guide as a reference to ensure consistent scoring aligned with your organization's standards.

When configuring a Custom Metric, simply select one or more documents from your Library to use as scoring guidelines. The AI will reference these documents when evaluating and scoring each deal.

#### New Metrics
We've added three new default Custom Metrics to help you better evaluate deal dynamics:
- **Customer Fit**: Measures how well your product aligns with the customer's needs and requirements
- **Messaging Fit**: Assesses how effectively your messaging resonates with the customer's pain points and objectives
- **Messaging Accuracy**: Evaluates how accurately your messaging reflects your product's capabilities and differentiators

#### Filtering by Metric Scores
You can now filter deals based on their Custom Metric scores. This allows you to quickly identify deals that meet specific scoring criteria, such as low sales execution or low product fit.

When building datasets or running analyses, simply apply filters based on the desired metric scores to focus on the most relevant deals for your insights. These filtering capabilities allow you to build smarter alerting workflows so you can quickly flag opportunities that need attention.

### Web Search Tool (Experimental)
We've introduced an experimental Web Search tool that allows you to augment AI analyses with real-time information from the web. This is particularly useful for gathering the latest competitive intelligence, market trends, and industry news that may not be present in your existing deal data.

You can ask the Agent to find new material on the internet, and then click to save it the right competitor's CI library.
This improves the customization of your newsletters and other workflows.

We've significantly upgraded the Data Analyst Agent in Chat, enabling powerful analytics across your entire deal library.

Key improvements include:

- **AI-Powered Pattern Recognition**: Leverages outputs from AI Deal Reviews to surface patterns in competition, product fit, sales execution, and more

- **Recursive Segmentation**: Automatically segments data to drill down into trends and outliers

Coming soon:

### Slack Admin & Usage Analytics
New admin controls and usage analytics for the Hindsight Slack Assistant.

Key capabilities include:
- **Custom System Prompt**: Configure a custom system prompt to tailor AI responses in Slack to your team's tone, terminology, and guidelines
- **Competitive Intel Toggle**: Control whether the Slack Assistant can search and surface competitive intelligence data
- **Usage Analytics**: Track how many questions are being asked in Slack over time, identify top topics and competitors being queried, and see which team members are engaging most with the assistant

### Variables in Recipes
Recipes now support configurable variables, making it easier to create flexible, reusable analysis workflows that can be quickly customized at runtime.

Key capabilities include:
- **Text Variables**: Add simple text inputs that users fill in when running a recipe (e.g., competitor name for a **Top Objections** recipe)
- **Filter Variables**: Let users apply Deal filters at runtime (e.g., filter a **Product Gaps** report to your Enterprise segment, or filter a **Win Themes** report to deals won in the last quarter)

This makes Recipes more accessible for teams who want standardized workflows with the flexibility to adapt each run to their specific needs.
You can configure variables when building or remixing a recipe in the Recipe Builder. You can make variables required or optional, and set default values for both text and filter variables.
