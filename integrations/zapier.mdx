## Overview

Hindsight's Zapier integration allows you to send the outputs of your workflows to thousands of apps without needing us to build a native integration. For example, you can automatically create a slide deck in [Gamma.ai](https://gamma.ai) from a Hindsight workflow output.

<Note>
The Zapier integration is currently invite-only. [Request access in the app](https://app.usehindsight.com/settings/integrations) to get started. You'll receive an invite to use the Zapier integration in your email.
</Note>

## Create a Zap

When creating a Zap, select **Hindsight** as the Trigger app. You should see Hindsight (1.3.0) as the version.


## Authenticate your Hindsight account

Generate an API key from the [API Keys](https://app.usehindsight.com/settings/keys) page. Paste your API key when connecting your Hindsight account to the Accounts tab in Zapier to authenticate.

<img alt="API Keys" classname="block" src="/images/api-keys.png" />

## Configure your Action

Select your destination app (e.g., Gamma.ai, Google Slides, Notion) and map the workflow output fields to your action. You'll typically want to use the Report Content field. 
This allows you to automatically send Hindsight workflow results to your connected apps.

<img alt="Zap Action" classname="block" src="/images/zap-configure-action.png" />

## Test and Enable

Test your Zap to make sure everything is working correctly, then turn it on to start automatically sending workflow outputs to your connected apps.

## Using Hindsight as an Action

You can also use Hindsight as an Action to send data *into* Hindsight from other apps. This is useful for importing documents from meeting notetakers and other sources.

### Create a Zap with Hindsight Action

Select your trigger app (e.g., a meeting notetaker like Fathom), then select **Hindsight** as the Action app. Choose the **Create Document** action to create a new document in your Hindsight account whenever the Zap is triggered.

<img alt="Zap Action" classname="block" src="/images/zap-action.png" />

### Map your fields

Map the fields from your trigger app to the fields in Hindsight. This will allow you to send data from your trigger app to Hindsight when the Zap is triggered.

<img alt="Zap Action Settings" classname="block" src="/images/zap-action-settings.png" />

### Test and turn on your Zap

Test your Zap to make sure everything is working correctly. This will send a test document to your Hindsight account. Once verified, turn on your Zap to start sending data.

<img alt="Deal Documents" classname="block" src="/images/deal-documents-fathom.png" />
