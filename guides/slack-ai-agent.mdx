Hindsight's Slack Assistant gives sales reps instant access to competitive intelligence and deal insights without leaving their workflow. It's designed to deliver quick, verified answers from your curated knowledge base — not exploratory analysis.

---

## How It Works

Add the Hindsight bot to your Slack workspace and interact in two ways:

- **DM the bot directly** for private conversations
- **Use `@hindsight` in any channel** where the bot has been added

The assistant searches your verified knowledge base — analyzed deals, approved sales assets, and competitive intelligence — and returns sourced answers.

**Quick Commands:**
- `@hindsight <your question>` — Ask anything about competitors, deals, or enablement content
- `/assets [competitor name]` — List all available assets for a specific competitor with direct links

---

## What the Slack Assistant Can Access

The Slack Assistant is scoped to verified, production-ready content:

**✅ Available:**
- Analyzed and verified deals
- Sales assets and battlecards from your [knowledge base](/guides/knowledge-base-setup)
- Approved competitive intelligence

**❌ Not Available:**
- Raw, unverified competitive data
- Full CRM database access
- Unprocessed transcripts or notes

This keeps answers consistent and trustworthy. For deeper analysis and full data access, use the [in-app chat](https://app.usehindsight.com/chat).

## Managing the Slack Assistant

Admins can monitor and control the Slack Assistant from [the control panel](https://app.usehindsight.com/slack-assistant).

**Activity Logs**

Track how your team uses the assistant:
- View all questions — who asked what, when
- Monitor which competitors are being researched most
- Identify unanswered questions to find content gaps

Use these logs to understand what intel your team needs and spot gaps in your [knowledge base](/guides/knowledge-base-setup).

**Settings & Configuration**

Control the assistant's behavior:
- **Custom Instructions** — Add guidelines for tone, messaging, and response style
- **Tool Access Control** — Toggle which capabilities the assistant can use (deal search, competitive intel, asset generation, etc.)

This lets you fine-tune what reps can access based on data readiness. If certain sources aren't ready for production, disable them until they're verified.

## Common Use Cases

**Competitor Questions**

Get quick answers from your competitive intelligence library:

> "Does Competitor A support SSO integration?"
> 
> "How does Competitor B typically price their enterprise plan?"
> 
> "What are our main strengths vs. Competitor C in the mid-market?"

The assistant shows sources from your battlecards and intel library, then provides a synthesized answer.

**Deal Insights**

Access insights from analyzed deals:

> "What objections came up in the Acme Corp deal?"
> 
> "Why did we win the GlobalTech deal against Competitor X?"
> 
> "What pain points did the prospect mention in our last call?"

The assistant searches verified deal analysis and returns key themes, objections, and outcomes.

**Asset Generation**

Create content for active deals:

> "Create a battlecard for competing against Competitor A in the MegaCorp deal"
> 
> "Write an email explaining our differentiators vs. Competitor B"
> 
> "Generate talking points for handling pricing objections against Competitor X"

The assistant pulls relevant deals and competitive intel, then generates ready-to-use content.

## Best Practices

**Be specific.** The more context you provide, the better the answer.
- Good: "What pricing objections came up against Competitor A?"
- Better: "What pricing objections came up in enterprise deals against Competitor A in Q3?"

**Use `/assets` for quick discovery.** Before asking a question, check what assets already exist for a competitor. The command shows everything available with direct links.

**Check the main platform if you need more.** The Slack Assistant is optimized for speed and verified answers. For deeper analysis, raw data access, or complex queries, use the [in-app chat](https://app.usehindsight.com/chat).
